Waking Nightmares

Every day I have nightmares.
Ones that gives even those without a care in the world,
fear.

The overwhelming fear
that, regardless of what they do,
Death draws near.
Now they only dream.

They dream
of a better future,
however impossible it may seem.
But, as time passes,
its becoming clear
that the dream is fleeting.

The dream is fleeting,
because they are losing their feeling on the inside.
They feel their life is not worth completing.

When they attempt to take life,
they may miss,
but they might plunge the knife into their chest.
If so, the pain will finally be released.

Finally,
the pain is gone.
