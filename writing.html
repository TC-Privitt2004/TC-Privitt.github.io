Echoing

The echo chamber of pain continues

only to remain unheard, unseen

no matter how loud, how flamboyant


They say “Oh you’re fine”

“You’re just a teen, be happy”

“Quit being dramatic”

But a lost life changes perspective

“I tried to help”

“I can’t believe it happened”

“I was the one who saw the signs”


Those closest weep

Those furthest give condolences

Those heartless give insincere prayers

Those full of heart help

THOSE WHO LISTEN PREVENT


But in the end,

it doesn’t matter.

Curtains close...

Fade to black...

The trigger pulls...

bang
Another Day Goes By

Another day goes by,

like a ticking clock going on forever...

Horrifying thoughts...

Terrible lies…

Lost lives…

I'm Not Okay

I smile and say,

"It's all good"

When I mean to say,

"I'm not okay"


False masks worn

to make everybody happy.

When that mask gets torn,

I finally say,

"I'm not okay"


I talk about

how I am so miserable.

Somehow still there's doubt when I say,

"I'm not okay"


Until it's too late.

Another lost life.

See the dead body and they finally notice

what I mean when I say,

"I'm not okay"
